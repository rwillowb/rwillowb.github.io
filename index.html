Introducing Queen Willow

Selfies📷

I have dedicated this site to my beloved (and pain in the butt) cat Willow 🐱

Willow was born May 2015 and had lived with 1 family prior to being surrendered to a shelter in early 2016. In June of 2016 I had visited the now closed animal shelter in Gatineau, QC and decided to adopt this little nugget of joy that day. Since then, Willow has been my loyal companion and I can't imagine where I'd be without her (and her many, many hairballs).

A few of Willow's favourite things

  • Naps
  • Treats
  • Sleeping on dads head
  • Taking smelly poops
Some of the things Willow loves are to run around like she snorted 6 grams of refined catnip and then crash on the rug shortly afterwards. She is truly a unique cat, and if you find yourself in a place where a cat may seem like the right companion for you I suggest researching if a cat is the right fit for you!
